Shipping, Returns & Order Information

Shipping Information

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver throughout South Africa, with a primary focus on Gauteng and surrounding provinces.

Shipping Methods

  • Standard Delivery: 3-5 business days
  • Express Delivery: 1-2 business days (select areas)
  • Local Pickup: Available in Pretoria

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and order size. Free shipping is available on orders over R500 within Gauteng.

Note: Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days. You will receive a tracking number once your order has been dispatched.

Return Policy

30-Day Return Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, you can return it within 30 days of receipt.

Return Conditions

  • Products must be unopened and in original packaging
  • Seals must be intact and unbroken
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase (order number) is required

How to Return

Contact us via WhatsApp at 081 022 2851 with your order number and reason for return. Our team will guide you through the return process.

Full refund issued once returned product is received and inspected

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used products (for hygiene and safety reasons)
  • Products damaged due to misuse

How to Order

Step 1: Browse Products

Explore our range of premium botanical supplements and select the products that suit your wellness needs.

Step 2: Add to Cart

Click "Add to Cart" on any product. You can adjust quantities in your cart before checkout.

Step 3: Checkout via WhatsApp

Click the "Checkout via WhatsApp" button in your cart. This will open a WhatsApp message with your order details.

Your order message will include:
  • Product names and quantities
  • Total order amount

Step 4: Confirm Your Order

Send the WhatsApp message to complete your order. Our team will respond with:

  • Payment instructions
  • Delivery details and shipping costs
  • Estimated delivery timeframe

Step 5: Make Payment

We accept various payment methods including bank transfer and instant EFT. Payment details will be provided via WhatsApp.

Your order will be processed immediately upon payment confirmation

Additional Information

Product Quality

All our botanical supplements are sourced from trusted suppliers and meet high quality standards. Each product is carefully selected to ensure purity and effectiveness.

Storage Instructions

Store products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep containers tightly closed when not in use.
